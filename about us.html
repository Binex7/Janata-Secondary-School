📘 About Us

Shree Janata Secondary School is a proud community institution in Shahidnagar Danda, Nawalparasi. Dedicated to quality education, we empower students with knowledge, ethics, and leadership.

Since its establishment, the school has been a beacon of learning for rural youth, helping them shape a bright future through education.
